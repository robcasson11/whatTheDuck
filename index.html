We're a high-energy acoustic duo packing a dance floor punch. We play all your favourite hits, from Robbie Williams to Oasis, Bon Jovi to Blink 182, Cher to Backstreet Boys, you name it! All performed with our unique spin: flamenco flair, rockin' riffs, and a touch of Nashville twang.

Sing along, dance the night away! We keep the energy high without overwhelming your guests. Our clever guitar effects, drum pedals, and a master's degree in the tambourine create a full sound with a minimal footprint, perfect for any venue.

We're all about you! Super punctual, flexible with requests, and fast on set-up, we make your event a celebration. Let's create an unforgettable night together!
